About EnnieCalc Multi-Functional App

This app is a Web-Based App that has been developed to simplify the work of students of navigating different portals available at Malawi University of Businnes and Applied SCiences (MUBAS). For example, SMIS, SFIMIS, E_Library and many more are some of the important portals that have been featured into this single package so that students will access them with just a single opening of the this App. Further more, some of the other funny and interesting programs that have also been embedded into this package are ; Salary Tax Calculator, Scientific Calculator, Subject Scheduler among others!
